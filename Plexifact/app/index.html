About

In today’s world, every business is an information-driven business.
PLEXIFACT’s SaaS data management platform empowers information-driven
businesses to find, understand, use and analyze the right data for better, faster
and more accurate business decisions.

PLEXIFACT was founded with the objective of bringing agile
enterprise data solutions used in top financial institutions to
a broader audience at a fraction of the cost.

We believe in Quality Information as the common language that streamlines
business processes. Our technology provides a complete data management foundation
to reduce the cost and complexity of deploying business intelligence solutions
for companies of any size.

PLEXIFACT combines the power of sophisticated architecture
and machine learning with human insight to automatically capture information
about what the data describes, where the data comes from,
who’s using it and how it’s used.

Capabilities

PLEXIFACT partners with clients to deploy targeted solutions that
take full ownership of our clients’ entire data management process. Integration
with PLEXIFACT brings intelligence and automation to any data-driven process
within your organization — from routine activities to analysis
and reporting on the most complex information assets.

Our modular scalable solutions can configure any structured or semi-structured
data from any disparate source (public, proprietary or acquired) to create a holistic picture
of your internal information assets, the company, the industry and beyond. By designing
our solutions to provide immediate operational impact and deliver long-term enterprise value,
you can now use data to discover hidden revenue opportunities, measure the impact of your
initiatives, see a full picture of the health of your company, account for risk and make
informed business decisions that drive profitability of your enterprise.
